START SAVING ENERGY TODAY!

ELECTRICITY USAGE

Lights

  • Use LED Light Bulbs; they use less energy.
  • Use natural light more; open curtains and blinds to let light in.

Electrical Appliances

  • Turn off electrical appliances when they are not in use.
  • Plug out electrical devices that are turned off; they still draw power.

WATER CONSERVATION

Taps and Pipes

  • Always ensure that taps are fully turned off after use.
  • Replace any leaky taps and ensure they are in good condition.

Gardening

  • Set up buckets/containers around your yard to collect rainwater to water plants.
  • Setup an irrigation system to efficiently water your plants/crops.

RENEWABLE ENERGY

Solar Panels

  • Install solar panels once enough research and consideration has been done.
  • Ensure panels are placed where most likely to get the most impact from the sun.

Residential Wind Turbines

  • Small scale wind turbines can also be used to store gain renewable energy.
  • Best suitable if you live an area frequented by winds.

BEHAVIOURAL CHANGES

Behaviour with Technology

  • Ensure refrigerators doors are closed tightly after use and the seal is working properly.
  • Always remember to turn off lights when leaving a or in unoccupied rooms.

Behaviour with Travel

  • Coordinate with friends, family and neighbours to carpool and reduce emissions.
  • Take public transport, or walk, instead of driving to close locations.

ENERGY CONSERVATION IN THE COMMUNITY

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Establish programmes in your community to encourage the community to recycle.
  • Set up containers around the community to encourage residents to recycle.

Community Gardens

  • Set up plots of land where residents can come together and grow vegetation.
  • Assign residents different roles in maintaing the garden.
